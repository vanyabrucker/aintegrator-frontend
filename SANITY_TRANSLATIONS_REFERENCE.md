## HOW TO USE THIS GUIDE

1. **Go to your Sanity Studio** (localhost:3333)
2. **Open each document** (Site Settings, Home Page, About Page, etc.)
3. **For each field listed above:**
   - Click the English field (en) → Copy the English translation from this guide
   - Click the Deutsch field (de) → Copy the German translation
   - Click the Français field (fr) → Copy the French translation
   - Click the Italiano field (it) → Copy the Italian translation
4. **Verify** that all 4 languages are DIFFERENT (not identical)
5. **Publish** the document
6. **Test in Angular app** - Switch languages to verify text changes
